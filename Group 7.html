PURPOSE

Most upcoming college students struggle in choosing the course they want to take in college, and choosing the school where they want to apply and study. Our website, Acadpath will help senior high school students in choosing the right path for them. Our main purpose is to provide information about the different cources that align with their perspective strands (STEM, ABM, GAS, TVL, ICT, etc).

PROS & CONS
Our website provides the pros and cons they might want to consider if they choose a certain course. We will also provide videos, for example "Things you need to know before taking engineering". This way, users can make sure they will look at other aspects too when choosing their career path.

SCHOOL RECOMMENDATIONS
Our site will also provide lists of schools that prioritizes certain fields, and link the application form of them so the students can see it and apply to them.

QUIZ
Aside from informations we provide, our users can also take quizzes that will show results of recommendations and suggestion courses that align with their answer (examples: it aligns with their hobbies, strength, talent, etc)

LETTER FOR FUTURE SELF
Our site also has an option where the users can send a letter for the future selves. A letter that they can customize and choose a date where they will receive it in the future. This will motivate them and will remind them what their dreams are when they are in senior high school.
